Frequently Asked Questions

MyLottoCoin is a fully permissionless DeFi-powered play-to-earn lottery DApp, the world’s first fully decentralized and transparent cryptocurrency lottery operated by a smart contract on Binance Smart Chain (BSC), leveraging the advantages of blockchain technology that offers a practical approach to simplifying the current online lottery model and fixing its defects by enabling fast, reliable, and tamper-proof technology access to players, while maintaining the traditional elements.

A Smart contract is simply a program stored on a blockchain that runs when predetermined conditions are met. They typically are used to automate the execution of an agreement so that all participants can be immediately certain of the outcome, without any intermediary's involvement or time loss.

You don’t need to Sign Up or create an account to play lottery on this platform. By choosing your numbers and paying the amount in BNB directly from your wallet, you are automatically participating in the lottery. Your wallet address and your chosen tickets’ numbers are recorded on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) without any third party involvement.

You don’t need to deposit any funds and you have the full control of your crypto assets. Since no sign up is required to play MyLottoCoin, your ticket purchases and payments are immediate and instant anytime you feel lucky to play.

To play lottery, you need first to connect your wallet to MyLottoCoin DApp by clicking “Connect to a Wallet” and choosing your preferred wallet. Once your wallet is connected to MyLottoCoin DApp, simply select five main numbers from 1-69 and a single Powerball number from 1-26. You can choose your numbers manually, or with the Quick Pick option. You can add up to 5 entries per ticket and when you feel lucky with your numbers, click 'Pay Now' and you will be directed to your wallet to make the payment in BNB (BSC). Once the transfer is done, your tickets and numbers are automatically entered and recorded in BSC smart contract for that specific draw.

Results are based on US Powerball drawings which are held every Monday, Wednesday and Saturday at 10:59 p.m. ET. The smart contract captures the draw results and winning numbers from US Powerball, that is instant and live, and checks with all purchased tickets and numbers. Results will appear on our homepage as well as on the Results page as soon as they are published. If you win a prize, the payout is instantly done in BNB to your wallet by smart contract and no third party/claim is involved in this process (Claimless).

US Powerball is one of the most popular and trusted lotteries in the U.S and one of the biggest in the world and many lotto players are familiar with the game and rules. On the other hand, MyLottoCoin wants to avoid any ambiguity and opacity around self-random number generations and confusion around smart contract logic in order to provide a transparent lottery experience.

MyLottoCoin is an independent and fully decentralized lottery and it’s neither associated nor linked to any lottery corporation. Unlike other crypto lottery, draws are not done by random numbers generated by smart contract or any instant draws, rather the smart contract captures the draw results and winning numbers from official US Powerball draws.

MyLottoCoin lottery is operated by a fully permissionless smart contract guaranteeing the full function of a transparent and independent lottery, without any human and third party involvement, which makes it the most trusted and secured online lottery in the world.

MyLottoCoin development team is committed to include more lotteries, among the world’s most famous lottery games, in near future.

For every 5 lottery tickets in a single purchase, 10 MYL tokens will be automatically airdropped into the player's wallet; the same BSC wallet address the payment is made to purchase the tickets.

To manage MYL assets, players need to add MYL token contract address as a custom token in their BSC (BEP-20) compatible wallet. MYL Token (BEP-20) contract address is: https://bscscan.com/address/0xD9A2267C10aBE89D47C3B74A1Aa033BD2190Ea30

On top of being a lottery DApp, MyLottoCoin’s DNA comprises a staking mechanism but unlike other staking projects, you have full control of your crypto assets in your own personal wallet!

Hodlers of minimum MYL100 throughout each reward quarter, receive 10% of total sale on MyLottoCoin DApp in BNB, based on the average of daily snapshots of MYL balance and in proportion to the tokens in circulation in that particular quarter.
