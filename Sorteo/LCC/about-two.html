Why MyLottoCoin

By empowering players to be always a part of this fair game and win, there are already millions of reasons why you should be playing with MyLottoCoin lottery. But you don’t need a million reasons. All you need is eight - and every one of them’s a winner:

World Record Jackpot

85% of lottery ticket sales go to the prize pool. MyLottoCoin allocates the largest percentage to the jackpot and winners than any traditional and online lottery around the world.

Transparent
 

The Smart contract is public, and anyone with Internet access can see the source code, payments, Balance, and prize payouts and all transactions at all times.

Reliable
 

Instant payments are guaranteed by smart contract with no human or third party involvement, benefiting the safe and secure blockchain environment.

Global Access
 

Anyone who has Internet access, a BNB (BSC) crypto wallet and some BNB balance, can play with no country, and citizenship restrictions.

Play-to-Earn
 

For every 5 tickets purchased in one time, you will automatically receive 10 MYL tokens airdropped to your BNB (BSC) wallet by the smart contract. Learn about MYL.

Anonymous
 

Unlike traditional and online lotteries, MyLottoCoin winners remain anonymous and no identity can be exposed at any stage. No sign-up/ registration is required.

Instant Payouts
 

Payouts are instant in BNB to the winners’ BNB (BSC) wallet address; the same wallet address the payment was made to purchase the tickets, without any need to claim.

The first of its kind

A Fully Decentralized and Transparent crypto-lottery operated by a smart contract which makes it the most trusted, independent, autonomous, anonymous, and secure lottery in the world!
