How to play

To play on MyLottoCoin Dapp, whether you browse from desktop or mobile, you need first to connect your wallet to MyLottoCoin DApp by clicking “Connect to a Wallet” and choosing your preferred wallet. Once your wallet is connected to MyLottoCoin DApp, simply select five main numbers from 1-69 and a single Powerball number from 1-26. You can choose your numbers manually, or with the Quick Pick option

You can add up to 5 entries per ticket and when you feel lucky with your numbers, click 'Pay Now' and you will be directed to your wallet to make the payment in BNB (BSC). Once the transfer is done, your tickets and numbers are automatically entered and recorded in BSC smart contract for that specific draw.


Ticket sales for the current draw is closed two (2) hours before the drawing countdown ends via a permissionless smart contract logic. Every purchased ticket within this time span, is autonomously considered for the next draw.

Reward Program (Staking+)

MyLottoCoin has introduced a community-centric reward program reconceptualizing the lottery model by a tokenized play-to-earn initiative, as the first of its kind, and empowering players to always be part of the game and win.

To learn more, visit www.mylottocoin.io

In a nutshell, For 5 lottery tickets in a single purchase, 10 MYL tokens will be automatically airdropped into the player’s wallet; the same wallet address the payment is made to purchase the tickets.

Hodlers of minimum MYL100 throughout each reward quarter, receive 10% of total ticket sale in BNB, in proportion to the circulating tokens, autonomously every 3 months and irrespective of playing games. Play Responsibly

MyLottoCoin encourage everyone to play responsibly and only play as much as they can afford. Since MyLottoCoin is totally decentralized and anonymous, the development team doesn’t have any control on players’ identity, ticket purchases, funds, and prize payouts, therefore we highly encourage players to consider the age limit and taxes wherever it applies based on where they live. MyLottoCoin is neither responsible nor can be held accountable for violating any applicable local, national or international law.
